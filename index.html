Craftsmanship

The keepsake creation process

1. Photo Submission

Send us your pet's photos. Our artists study their fur pattern, body shape, and facial features to plan the sculpting.

2. Hand-Sculpting

We sculpt the clay model from scratch. We send you photos of the unpainted clay structure for your preview and approval.

3. Firing & Glazing

The piece is carefully hand-painted with ceramic colors, glazed, and kiln-fired to lock in the colors permanently.
