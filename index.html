Our Philosophy

Beauty in imperfection

We believe objects should carry stories. In a world of mass production, we invite you to embrace the subtle irregularities of hand-crafted pottery. The slight spiral of the potter's fingers, the variation in glaze color, and the raw sandy base make each item singular and personal.

The Studio Craft

The pottery process

1. Throwing

Centering and shaping the raw, moist clay body on the spinning wheel. Hand movements determine the flow of the silhouette.

2. Glazing

After bisque firing, we dip or brush the pieces in custom-mixed glaze formulas. Interior glazing ensures water retention.

3. Kiln Firing

Placing pieces in high-temperature kilns. The chemical heat reaction bonds raw oxides, creating speckled textures.
